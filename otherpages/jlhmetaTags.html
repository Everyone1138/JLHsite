About JLH

Founded in 2014 by a group of veteran game developers who grew up with the 16-bit and 32-bit eras, Jupiter Lighthouse is dedicated to keeping the spirit of classic gaming alive.

We combine modern development techniques with authentic pixel art and chiptune music to create experiences that feel both nostalgic and fresh.

All our games are developed with genuine 32-bit architecture limitations in mind, ensuring authentic performance across all platforms.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

You can report bugs by emailing our support team at support@Jupiter LightHouse.com. Please include as much detail as possible about the issue, including screenshots if applicable, the device you're using, and steps to reproduce the bug.

We love hearing creative ideas from our community! While we can't promise to implement every suggestion, we do review all submissions. Please send your ideas to ideas@Jupiter LightHouse.com with "Game Idea" in the subject line.

Check our Careers page for open positions. We're always looking for talented pixel artists, developers, and game designers who share our passion for retro gaming. Even if we don't have an open position that matches your skills, feel free to send us your portfolio at careers@Jupiter LightHouse.com.

We announce release dates on our social media channels and newsletter when we're confident about them. Game development is unpredictable, and we prefer to take the time needed to polish our games rather than rush them out. Subscribe to our newsletter to be the first to know!
